Overview

Hearding Kittens is a medical scheduling application designed for healthcare professionals working in NICU and Morning Rounder teams. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Schedule information and work block assignments
  • Coworker names and scheduling preferences
  • Schedule requests and notes
  • General notes and reminders

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device information for app functionality
  • Usage analytics to improve app performance

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide schedule management functionality
  • To sync your data across your devices using CloudKit
  • To generate schedule reports and statistics
  • To send reminders and notifications
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Your personal schedule data is stored locally on your device and synced using Apple's CloudKit service. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

The app may integrate with:

  • Apple CloudKit for data synchronization
  • Apple EventKit for reminders functionality
  • ChatGPT API for schedule optimization assistance

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy or with your explicit consent.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for healthcare professionals and is not designed for children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Users will be notified of significant changes through the app.
