Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my schedules across devices?

Hearding Kittens uses Apple's CloudKit to automatically sync your data across all your Apple devices signed in to the same iCloud account.

Is my scheduling data secure?

Yes! All your data is stored securely using Apple's CloudKit service and remains private to your iCloud account.

Can I export my schedules?

Yes, you can generate PDF reports of your schedules directly from the app.

What devices are supported?

Hearding Kittens is available for iPhone, iPad, and Mac, with seamless synchronization between all platforms.
