Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my schedules across devices?

Hearding Kittens uses Apple's CloudKit to automatically sync your data across all your Apple devices signed in to the same iCloud account. Make sure you're signed in to iCloud on all devices.

Is my scheduling data secure?

Yes! All your data is stored securely using Apple's CloudKit service and remains private to your iCloud account. We don't have access to your personal scheduling information.

Can I export my schedules?

Yes, you can generate PDF reports of your schedules directly from the app to share or print as needed.

What devices are supported?

Hearding Kittens is available for iPhone, iPad, and Mac, with seamless synchronization between all Apple platforms.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Please send us an email with details about the issue or feature request. We appreciate your feedback!

Is there a user manual?

The app includes built-in help and intuitive design. For specific questions, please contact our support team.
